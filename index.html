About the Theme

Join us for a time-themed hackathon, where participants will delve into various aspects of time, such as time management, time travel, time zones, timekeeping, and the history and future of time. We encourage you to think creatively and develop innovative solutions to time-related challenges.

Example Products

  • Develop a time-tracking app
  • Create an algorithm to optimize schedules
  • Build a time zone converter
